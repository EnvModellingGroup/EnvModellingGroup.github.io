People

PI: Jon Hill

Jon solves large-scale environmental problems using computational fluid dynamics and other software models. He have a diverse research background that spans ocean biogeochemistry, sedimentary modelling, biogeography, hazards and tidal dynamics. His primary focus is in palaeo-tsunamis and palaeo-tides at the moment.
